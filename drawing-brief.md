# Portative Organ Drawing Brief

This packet has no released drawings. `visual-output-register.csv` lists
`drawings/windchest-layout.dxf` as a planned, not-present artifact
(`PO-DXF-PLAN-001`), pending a measured windchest, key, pallet, and pipe-foot
layout.

Required first drawings, once the non-playing wind-system mule
(`PO-BOM-016`) is measured:

- Wind path schematic: bellows, reservoir, windchest, pallets, pipe feet, with
  qualitative flow direction only until pressures are measured.
- Windchest plan view: channel layout, pallet locations, and pipe-foot
  positions for the single first-rank stop.
- Pallet detail: travel, facing material, and seating reference, once a mule
  proves a workable geometry.
- Key row side view: key pivot, travel, guide, and stop geometry, once an
  ergonomic mockup exists.
- Case/support concept sketch: candidate strap, lap, or table support modes
  for the handling mockup in `validation.csv` (`VAL-008`).

## Authority Notes

- No drawing in this brief exists yet; this file records what is needed, not
  what has been produced.
- No pipe length, pipe diameter, wind pressure, or tuning value may appear in
  any drawing until it traces to a measured mule or a reviewed design table.
  See `design.md` V5 Boundary and `decision-record.md` Authority Boundary.
- `cad/portative-organ.scad` does not exist in this packet (see
  `cad/README.md` for the honest no-scad gap record); any future CAD source
  must cite the same measured provenance before it can back a drawing.
- Voicing details (languid position, flue opening, mouth cut-up, toe-hole and
  wind-supply detail at the pipe foot) are out of scope for this drawing
  brief and for any future CAD master; they remain a separate voicing study.
